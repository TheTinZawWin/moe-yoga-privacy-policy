Moe Yoga ("we", "our", "us") operates the Moe Yoga mobile application (the "App").

This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information Collection and Use

We do not collect personal information when you use our App, except as described in this Privacy Policy. We use your data to provide and improve the App. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data:

  • Login Information: We store login data locally on your device. This includes your email address and authentication tokens used for logging into the App.
  • Face ID: If you enable Face ID, we use the biometric data solely for authentication purposes. This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible by us.
  • Gmail Login/Signup: When you use Gmail to log in or sign up, we collect your email address and authentication tokens. This data is used only for authentication purposes.

Non-Personal Data:

  • Device Information: We may not collect information about your device, including its model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information. This information is used to improve the App and provide a better user experience.
  • Usage Data: We may not collect information about how you use the App, such as the features you interact with, the pages you visit, and the actions you take. This data is used to analyze and improve the App.

Network Access

The App requires network access to stream online yoga classes and access certain features. This may involve data usage depending on your mobile data or Wi-Fi connection.

Data Security

We are committed to ensuring that your information is secure. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard the information we collect and prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your personal data with third parties except as necessary to provide the services offered by the App or as required by law.
